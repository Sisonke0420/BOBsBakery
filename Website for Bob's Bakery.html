BOB's Bakery©



BOB’s Bakery is a proudly local, family-owned bakery established in 2003 by founder Mr. Bob Mokoena in the heart of Bethlehem, Free State. For over two decades, the bakery has been a cornerstone of the community, known for its freshly baked bread, pastries, and warm customer service. With a strong reputation built on consistency, quality, and tradition, BOB’s Bakery has become the preferred supplier of baked goods for households, schools, businesses, and local events in the area.
