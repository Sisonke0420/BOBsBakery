BOB's Bakery©



BOB’s Bakery is a proudly local, family-owned bakery established in 2003 by founder Bob Mokoena in the town of Bethlehem, Free State. For over a decade, the bakery has been a main pastry supplier of the community, known for its freshly made bread and inviting customer service. With a strong reputation built on consistency, quality, and tradition, BOB’s Bakery has become the preferred supplier of baked goods for households, schools, businesses, and local events in the area.
